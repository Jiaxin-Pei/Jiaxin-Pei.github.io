This course explores the design and development of practical applications powered by large language models (LLMs), including agentic systems that can plan, take actions, and use tools to complete real-world tasks. The course covers both LLM fundamentals—how LLMs work, their capabilities and limitations—and applied methods for building reliable systems, including prompting, retrieval-augmented generation, orchestration, safety, and evaluation.
